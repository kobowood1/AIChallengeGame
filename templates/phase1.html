Your Role as Policy Maker

As a member of the Republic of Bean parliament, your first task is to craft your personal vision for refugee education reform. For each of the seven policy domains below, select one option that you believe best addresses the needs of refugee students while considering budget constraints.

Remember: Your choices must stay within the 14-unit budget limit, and you need to select a mix of different option levels (you cannot choose all level 1s, all level 2s, or all level 3s).

After completing your selections, you'll engage in deliberation with four other citizens who have diverse perspectives on education policy. Through discussion and voting, you'll collectively decide on the final policy package.
